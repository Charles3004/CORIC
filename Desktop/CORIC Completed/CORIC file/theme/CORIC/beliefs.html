Fundamental Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God. It is the final authority for faith and conduct, revealing God's will for salvation and daily living.

Read More

The Trinity & Jesus Christ

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Jesus Christ, God's Son, became man, died for our sins, rose again, and is coming back as King.

Read More

Salvation, The Church & The Holy Spirit

We believe salvation comes by grace through faith in Christ alone. The Holy Spirit empowers believers to live holy lives. The Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, teach, and reach the world.

Read More

Baptism, The Lord’s Supper & Christ’s Return

We practice water baptism as a sign of faith, and observe the Lord’s Supper in remembrance of Christ’s sacrifice. We believe in the imminent return of Jesus to reign and judge the world.

Personal Testimonies

Testimony of Divine Healing – Sister Amanda O.

“I came to The City of Refuge after being told by doctors that I would never walk again unaided. During one of the healing services, the power of God hit me so strongly I stood up without help and walked for the first time in 3 years! The God of this house is real. His power doesn’t take time — it only takes God!”

Testimony of Marital Restoration – Mr. & Mrs. Daniel and Grace U

“Our marriage was on the brink of divorce. Bitterness, silence, and anger had taken over our home. But one encounter at the ‘Family Revival Night’ at The City of Refuge altered everything. Through the ministration, our hearts were healed, and the Spirit of God restored our love. Today, we mentor other couples from our pain turned testimony!”

Testimony of Freedom from Addiction – Brother Michael A

“I was deep in addiction for 8 years—alcohol, drugs, and sexual immorality. Friends and family gave up on me. But the power of God met me at an encounter service here. I fell to my knees, weeping uncontrollably. From that day, the taste of those things vanished. It's been two years now — clean, saved, and serving God. Indeed, no life is beyond God's touch!”

Testimony of Financial Breakthrough – Sister Faith N

“I had been jobless for 5 years with doors shutting in every direction. At The City of Refuge, I learned that my solution wasn’t time, it was God. During a prophetic service, the man of God declared a 24-hour miracle for forgotten files. That same week, I was called for a federal job I had applied for 3 years prior! Today, I’m not just employed—I’m a kingdom financier!”
